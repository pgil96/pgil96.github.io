About Us

Your trusted partner in commodities brokerage

Based in Spain, Horsan Brokers Company SL is an independent brokerage firm specializing in petroleum commodities. We act as a bridge between producers and buyers, facilitating efficient and transparent transactions across global markets.

With over 15 years of experience, we bring deep market knowledge, an extensive network of counterparties, and a commitment to professional integrity in every deal we structure.

  • Crude oil & refined petroleum products
  • Direct access to producers and end-buyers
  • Full KYC compliance and due diligence
What We Do

Our Services

We facilitate the efficient flow of petroleum commodities between counterparties across the globe.

Crude Oil Brokerage

Connecting crude oil suppliers with refineries and trading companies. We handle term contracts and spot trades across multiple grades and origins.

Refined Products

Gasoline, diesel, jet fuel, fuel oil, naphtha and more. We source and match refined product flows to meet your procurement or offtake needs.

International Trade

We coordinate counterparty introductions, assist in contract structuring, and support smooth cargo execution across international markets.
